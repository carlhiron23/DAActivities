# Equality & Diversity Policy

D A Activities is committed to promoting equality, valuing diversity and fostering an inclusive environment for participants, staff, volunteers and visitors. We oppose discrimination of any kind and work to ensure fair treatment for all.

## Purpose

To set out our commitment to equality, diversity and inclusion (EDI), and to describe how we will prevent discrimination and promote equal opportunities.

## Scope

This policy applies to everyone who works with or uses D A Activities services, including staff, volunteers, contractors, trustees, participants and visitors.

## Policy Commitments

- We will treat people fairly and with respect regardless of age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage and civil partnership, pregnancy and maternity, race, religion or belief, sex, or sexual orientation.
- We will take positive steps to remove barriers to participation and ensure reasonable adjustments where needed.
- We will challenge discriminatory behaviour and language and address complaints promptly.

## Responsibilities

- Board & leadership: ensure policies, training and monitoring are in place.
- Managers: apply the policy in recruitment, supervision and day-to-day activities.
- Individuals: act respectfully, report concerns, and participate in training.

## Recruitment & Selection

Recruitment processes will be fair, based on job-related criteria, and monitored for equal opportunity. Reasonable adjustments will be offered during recruitment where required.

## Accessibility & Reasonable Adjustments

We will consider physical, sensory and communication needs when planning sessions and premises, and make reasonable adjustments to enable participation.

## Harassment & Complaints

Reports of harassment, discrimination or bullying will be taken seriously, investigated promptly and handled sensitively. Victims will be supported and appropriate corrective action taken.

## Monitoring & Review

We will monitor participation and recruitment data where appropriate to identify and address inequalities. This policy will be reviewed annually.

Contact: For EDI concerns or requests for adjustments, contact D A Activities leadership.
